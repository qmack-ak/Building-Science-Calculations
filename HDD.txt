Heating Degree Day - HDD, HDD65 or HDDxx
Cumulative difference between the mean daily temperature and a set temperature baseline, commonly 65Â°F. 
A Heating Degree Day implies colder outdoor temperatures are likely to influence the demand for indoor heating 
required info: temperature and/or HDD specific data

formula: HDD = base temperature - daily mean temperature (ex. 65F - 20F = 45HDD)

info: https://www.degreedays.net/
publicly available data: https://www.weather.gov/wrh/climate
