Cooling Degree Day - CDD, CDD65 or CDDxx
Cumulative difference between the mean daily temperature and a set temperature baseline, commonly 65Â°F. 
A Cooling Degree Day implies warmer outdoor temperatures are likely to influence the demand for indoor cooling 
required info: temperature and/or CDD specific data

formula: CDD = daily mean temperature - base temperature (ex. 90F - 65F = 25CDD)

info: https://www.degreedays.net/
publicly available data: https://www.weather.gov/wrh/climate
